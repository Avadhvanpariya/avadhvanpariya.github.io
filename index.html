Rangoli is a traditional Indian art form where colorful patterns are created on the ground, typically in front of homes, using materials like colored rice, dry flour, colored sand, or flower petals. It's a beautiful way to express creativity and add a festive touch to celebrations. Here are some tips and steps to help you learn and master the art of Rangoli:

Rangoli is a form of art that allows for a high degree of personal expression.

Making Rangoli requires attention to detail and patience. Take your time to create intricate patterns, and enjoy the meditative aspect of the process.

Understand the cultural significance of Rangoli in various traditions, particularly in India.
