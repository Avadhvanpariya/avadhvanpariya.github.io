Home Cleaning

Home Cleaning" is the process of tidying and sanitizing living spaces to maintain a clean and healthy environment. It involves tasks such as dusting, vacuuming, mopping.

Sofa Cleaning

"Sofa cleaning" refers to the process of cleaning and maintaining upholstery, particularly sofas, to ensure they remain hygienic, aesthetically pleasing.

Chair Cleaning

CChair cleaning" involves the maintenance and sanitation of chairs to keep them clean, presentable, and free from dirt, stains, and allergens

bag Cleaning

Bag cleaning" refers to the process of maintaining and refreshing bags to keep them clean, hygienic, and looking their best. Bags, whether they're handbags, backpacks, or travel bags, can accumulate dirt

Carpet Cleaning

Carpet cleaning is the process of removing dirt, stains, and allergens from carpets to maintain their appearance and hygiene. Methods include vacuuming, spot cleaning for spills, steam cleaning

Shoes Cleaning

Shoe cleaning involves removing dirt, stains, and marks from footwear to maintain their appearance and hygiene. This process typically includes surface cleaning with a soft brush or cloth.
